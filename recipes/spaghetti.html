Spaghetti

This easy spaghetti and meat sauce recipe takes just a few minutes of prep and then a low simmer on the stove for a deep, meaty-flavored, homemade spaghetti for the best family favorite dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatos
  • Italian seasoning
  • Spaghetti noodles

Steps

  1. Cook noodles
  2. Brown the beef in a pan
  3. Pour in diced tomatos
  4. Pour in sauce
  5. Season the Sauce
  6. Let simmer for an hour
  7. Combine sauce and noodles
