Lasagna

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Herbs and Spices

Steps

  1. Put a small amount of sauce on the bottom of the pan
  2. Layer the bottom of the pan with noodles
  3. Add a thick layer of sauce
  4. Add a thick layer of cheese
  5. Repeat until the dish is full and bake at 350 for an hour
