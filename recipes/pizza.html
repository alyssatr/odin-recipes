Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni sausage

Steps

  1. Roll and shape pizza dough
  2. Pour sauce on dough
  3. Sprinkle generous amount of cheese on top of sauce
  4. Place pepperoni sausage on top of cheese
  5. Bake in pizza oven for 20 minutes
